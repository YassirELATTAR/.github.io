Volunteering Workshops (Learning English with Fun) at Descartes Association

Volunteering Workshops: Learning English with Fun

  • Category: Teaching & Activities
  • Client: Descartes Association of Culture & Education
  • Project date: February 27th, March 17th, & May 25th, 2022
  • Project URL: Association Instagram Page

As a volunteer for the Descartes Association of Education and Culture in Tangier, I had the pleasure of leading three workshops that focused on teaching English through fun activities. These workshops covered a range of engaging topics, including the use of games, songs, movies, reading, and art to help students improve their language skills. Through these interactive and dynamic sessions, we created a relaxed and enjoyable learning environment that fostered active participation and encouraged students to practice their English skills with confidence. It was a fulfilling experience to contribute to the education and cultural development of the local community and to see the positive impact these workshops had on the participants.
