LGEEK Workshops - 2023

Project information

  • Category: Volunteering Work
  • Client: L'GEEK Association
  • Project date: April 12th-13th, 2023
  • Project URL: L'GEEK Official

I'm excited to share that I recently volunteered with L'Geek Association to conduct programming workshops for high school students in Chefchaouen and Tetouan. These workshops, held on April 12th and 13th, aimed to introduce students to the digital world and provide them with their initial exposure to programming, alongside Photography, Digital Marketing, and Social Media components. It was an immensely gratifying experience contributing to the association's mission of reaching one million students and fostering essential digital skills. I look forward to further supporting L'Geek Association's mission.
The workshops were inspiring as we witnessed the students' enthusiasm and curiosity while exploring programming, many of whom had no prior exposure to it. We covered Python basics, including loops, conditionals, and functions, ensuring each student gained hands-on experience.
Being part of L'Geek Association's efforts to bring digital education to Morocco's youth fills me with pride. Equipping the next generation with essential skills for success is crucial, and I'm committed to supporting initiatives like these.
