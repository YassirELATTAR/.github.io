DNS Checker is a versatile Python application I developed for SV7.0, a startup company, to enhance their domain and IP management capabilities. This cross-platform tool efficiently retrieves comprehensive information about individual or multiple domains and IP addresses. Its key feature is the ability to analyze SPF records, uncovering all linked domains and IPs. This functionality allows users to maintain a good overview of their digital assets and identify potential security leaks or unexpected connections. The application supports both single and bulk queries, making it a powerful resource for monitoring large portfolios of domains and IP addresses. By providing deep insights into DNS relationships and SPF configurations, DNS Checker serves as an essential tool for maintaining digital security and managing online presence.
